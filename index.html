Introducing Roomate

A cohesive app that allows you to have your own messaging thread, allows for assigned chores/reminders & makes real time payments easier between your roommates.

Messaging with no distractions.

Our app allows you to get straight to the point with your rommates without any extra distractions. No status updates, no stories, just messages.

Payments just for home.

Make quick and easy payments to each other with only few taps of a button. You can request and send money to each other and the payments are all private to the household.

Be efficient with reminders

Groceries: Know exactly what you need for next time you go shopping. Mark important items, get reminders when nearby stores, and get instant fund transfer when you pick up an item listed by your rooommates. Groceries made simple.
