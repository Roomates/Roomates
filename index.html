RooMate

Communication among roommates has never been so easy.

With RooMates, you can assign chores, communicate with one another and make real time payments between your roommates.

Messaging with no distractions.

Our app allows you to get straight to the point with your rommates without any extra distractions. No status updates, no stories, just messages.

Payments you can trust

Make quick and easy payments to each other with only few taps of a button. You can easly request and send money to each other and the payments are all private to the household.

Be efficient with groceries

Know exactly what you need for next time you go shopping. Mark important items, get reminders when nearby stores, and get instant fund transfer when you pick up an item listed by your rooommates.
